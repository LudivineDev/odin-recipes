Matar Paneer

Description

  1. Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds, and let them splutter. Add chopped onions and saute until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute. Then, add tomato and cashew puree and cook until the oil separates.
  3. Add spices, Stir in turmeric, red chili powder, coriander powder, and cumin powder. Cook for another 2 minutes.
  4. Add green peas and water. Simmer for 5 minutes until the peas soften.
  5. Add paneer, Gently mix in paneerc cubes, garam masala, and kasuri methi. Simmer for 5 minutes. And ready to eat

Ingredients

Main Ingredients

  • 200g paneer, cubed
  • 1 cup green peas (fresh or frozen)
  • 1/2 cup water (adjust as needed)
  • 2 tbsp fresh cream (optional)
  • 1 tbsp kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)

For the Gravy

  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 1tbsp fresh ginger-garlic paste
  • 3 large tomatoes, a few cashews nuts pureed
  • 1 green chili, chopped (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • Salt to taste

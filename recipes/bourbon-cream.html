Bourbon Cream

Ingredients

  • Bourbon (50% ABV): 1 cup
  • Sweetened Condensed Milk: 300 ml (1 can)
  • Table Cream: 400 ml
  • Vanilla Extract: 1â€“2 tsp (optional, for flavor)
  • Instant Coffee or Cocoa Powder: 2 tsp (optional, for depth of flavor and color)

Description

  1. Add all ingredients to the blender and gently blend for a few minutes.
  2. Pour into a container and let it rest in the fridge for a few hours before serving, it should be a little thicker after cooling.

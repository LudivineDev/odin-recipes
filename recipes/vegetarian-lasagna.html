Vegetarian Lasagna

Ingrédients

For the roasted vegetables:

  • 2 medium zucchini, sliced into half-moons
  • 2 medium carrots, thinly sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 small eggplant, diced
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt & pepper
  • 1 tsp dried oregano (or Italian herbs)

For the tomato sauce:

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 large can (28 oz / 800 g) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp sugar (optional, balances acidity)
  • 1 tsp dried basil or oregano
  • Salt & pepper

For the béchamel (white sauce):

  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 4 tbsp flour
  • 4 cups milk (warm)
  • 1 pinch nutmeg (optional)
  • Salt & pepper

For assembling:

  • 12 lasagna noodles (pre-cooked or oven-ready)
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1 cup parmesan cheese, grated

Instructions

1. Roast the vegetables

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Toss zucchini, carrots, peppers, eggplant, and onion with olive oil, salt, pepper, and oregano.
  3. Spread on a baking sheet and roast for 20–25 minutes until tender and slightly caramelized.

2. Make the tomato sauce

  1. Heat olive oil in a saucepan. Add garlic, sauté 1 min.
  2. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, herbs, and sugar (if using).
  3. Simmer uncovered for 15-20 minutes. Season to taste.

3. Prepare the béchamel

  1. In a saucepan, melt butter. Whisk in flour and cook 1 min.
  2. Gradually add warm milk, whisking constantly until smooth and thickened (about 8 min).
  3. Season with salt, pepper, and nutmeg.

4. Assemble the lasagna

  1. Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  2. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce at the bottom.
  3. Layer noodles → roasted vegetables → tomato sauce → béchamel → sprinkle of cheese.
  4. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with béchamel and a generous topping of mozzarella + parmesan.

5. Bake

  1. Cover with foil and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 30 minutes.
  2. Remove foil and bake another 15 minutes until golden and bubbling.
  3. Rest 10 minutes before slicing.

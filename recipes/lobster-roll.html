Lobster Roll

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450 g) lobster meat, cut into large pieces
  • 3 tbsp (45 ml) mayonnaise
  • 2 tbsp finely diced celery
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh chives
  • 1 tsp chopped fresh tarragon
  • 1 tsp (5 ml) whole-grain mustard
  • 1 tsp (5 ml) lemon juice
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper
  • 8 hot dog buns
  • 3 tbsp (40 g) butter, softened
  • 8 Boston lettuce leaves
  • Mayonnaise, to taste

Description

  1. In a bowl, gently combine all the lobster ingredients. Season lightly with salt. Refrigerate until ready to serve.
  2. Spread the butter on the outer sides of the hot dog buns.
  3. In a non-stick skillet over medium-high heat, brown the buns on both sides.
  4. On a work surface, spread the mayonnaise on the insides of the buns, add lettuce leaves, and generously top with the lobster mixture.
  5. Serve immediately. The Lobster Rolls are delicious with Beer-Battered Onion Rings.
